TICKET 02

Contradictions and legal holes — the most serious part of the audit

🔴 legal.html demonstrably lies on two points.
  • "We don't ask for or store a password." — False since Jul 15: a password is mandatory for everyone (db/83) + Face ID unlock. Code: a mandatory gate after the NDA. One sentence in a document meant to build trust is refutable within 10 seconds of using the app.
  • GPS at check-in/out is not declared at all. The code (getCurrentPosition, db/63 location snapshot) records a one-off location at arrival/departure — that is employee location data, which both nDSG and GDPR class as sensitive. The Working Time section of legal.html talks about times and breaks, and says nothing about location. This is not cosmetics — in the first dispute with a pilot-kitchen employee it's a real problem.
The fix: 30 minutes of work on the legal.html text (password + a GPS sentence with purpose and voluntariness + a camera sentence for QR scanning). Alternative for GPS: switch the snapshot off until the trial.
