03 The conflict that harms the customer

A promised deletion against a legal duty to retain

Pilot agreement §8 promises data deletion within 30 days of ending. Swiss labour law requires an employer to retain working-time records for five years. If the kitchen uses Working Time as its record, your clause pushes it into a breach.

Agreement §8 — deletion
30 days
Labour Act — duty to retain
5 years
The fix is simple and belongs in the agreement before the first signature: deletion only after a confirmed export, the kitchen expressly takes responsibility for archiving its own records, and add an option for longer retention on its explicit request.
