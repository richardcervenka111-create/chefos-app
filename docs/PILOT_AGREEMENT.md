## 1. What ChefOS is

ChefOS is a **beta (pre-release) kitchen-management application**, still under active daily
development. Features can change, break, or be removed without notice during this pilot period.
It is not a finished, commercially supported product yet.

## 2. Data

- Everything the Kitchen enters into ChefOS — recipes, prep lists, ingredient prices, schedules,
  working-time logs, photos — **belongs to the Kitchen**.
- Data is stored on Supabase, a third-party managed database provider. The Developer does not
  sell, share, or use the Kitchen's data for any purpose other than running the app.
- Some features (photo-scanning a recipe or schedule, generating a recipe, translating a name,
  the Chef's Assistant chat) send the relevant text or image to Anthropic's Claude AI service for
  processing. No data is used to train AI models beyond what Anthropic's own standard API terms
  cover.
- The Kitchen can request an export of its data, or ask for it to be deleted, at any time.

## 3. Food-safety (HACCP) disclaimer

ChefOS can suggest shelf-life "use by" dates and generate food-safety labels based on
information entered into the app. **These are tools to assist the Kitchen's own food-safety
process — they are not a substitute for the Kitchen's own HACCP procedures, training, or
professional judgment.** The Kitchen remains solely responsible for verifying any date, label,
or temperature reading before relying on it. The Developer accepts no liability for food-safety
outcomes arising from use of the app during this pilot period.

## 4. No cost, no obligation

Participation in this pilot is **free and voluntary**. The Kitchen is not committing to become a
paying customer, and the Developer is not committing to any particular feature, timeline, or
level of support.

## 5. Ending the pilot

Either the Kitchen or the Developer may end this pilot **at any time, for any reason**, with no
penalty. On request, the Kitchen's data will be exported and/or deleted within a reasonable time.
