What we built

Today's tickets

Seven pieces went from nothing to working in one session, grouped the way a kitchen would group its own prep.

Core Recipe Engine

Phase 1a
  • Recipes moved off one browser's storage onto a real, shared database
  • All 173 existing recipes migrated, fully searchable and editable — not just the ones added after
  • Sign-in by email + one-time code, no password to manage
  • Favorites, personal notes, and ingredient-based scaling, all synced across devices

Ingredients & Pricing

Pulled from Phase 3
  • 535-item price book — European, Asian, and universal pantry — editable any time
  • Every recipe now shows an estimated batch cost, calculated automatically from matched ingredients
  • A line-by-line breakdown behind an "i" button, so the total is never a black box

Nutrition

Pulled from Phase 3
  • Calories, protein, carbs and fat recorded per ingredient
  • Calculated per recipe the same transparent way as cost, with its own breakdown

Origin, Season & Substitutes

Pulled from Phase 3
  • Where an ingredient comes from, when it's in season, what it can be swapped for
  • One tap from any ingredient line inside a recipe

Recipe Cross-Linking

Phase 1a
  • An ingredient that's really another recipe — a house sauce, a stock — links straight to it
  • Allergens flow through from the linked recipe automatically, not just from its name

Mise en Place

Still finding its shape
  • Per-station prep checklist — Hot Line, Entremetier, Garde Manger, Desserts, BQT, Lobby, Extra
  • Opening a station auto-fills it from the recipes tagged to it
  • Each task moves through TO DO → CHECK → FINISH

Order List

Pulled from Phase 3
  • Per-station ordering checklist pulled from the full ingredient book, grouped by category
  • Check an item, set a quantity and unit, save
What we built, round two

Later the same day — worked without check-ins

After a broad go-ahead, four more pieces closed out the rest of Phase 1a.

Full Recipe Format

Closes out Phase 1a
  • Every field from the original spec — equipment, chef notes, storage, shelf life, variations, scaling notes, plating, cross references, tags, yield, cuisine
  • Tucked into a collapsible "More details" section so the fast path stays fast

Real AI Photo-Scan

Phase 1b, done
  • Point a camera at a handwritten or printed recipe — Claude reads it, fixes spelling, standardizes terms, converts to metric
  • Your own Anthropic key, typed straight into the app, never seen by anyone else
  • Lands in the normal edit form for a look before it's saved — nothing goes in unreviewed

Mobile Hardening

Polish
  • Fixed the cost/nutrition panel forcing four columns on a phone screen
  • Long ingredient quantities can wrap instead of pushing the page wider than the screen
  • A site-wide safety net so this class of bug can't recur unnoticed

Data Quality Pass

Clean bill of health
  • Every price, nutrition value, and recipe quantity checked programmatically for impossible numbers
  • Nothing broken found — a few odd-looking calorie counts (wine, saké, vanilla extract) turned out to be correct, just carrying calories from alcohol
