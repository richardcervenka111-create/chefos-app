ChefOS — Swiss Market Overview

ChefOS

An operating system for professional kitchens, built for the Swiss hospitality market. Go-to-market is deliberately narrow: prove it in canton Bern first, then expand — not a global launch plan. Built solo, in production, validated against a real pilot kitchen (Burrito Bandito & Lido).

Financials & monetization

Subscription tiers map directly to the product's own user tiers (Home Cook / Chef Pro / Restaurant), priced in CHF for the Swiss market. Go-to-market starts in canton Bern — the pilot kitchen's own canton — before expanding to neighbouring cantons and eventually German-speaking Switzerland. A future B2B layer — Swiss suppliers transacting through the platform — is vision-stage, not built.
